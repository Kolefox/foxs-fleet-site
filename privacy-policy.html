Privacy Policy

Fox’s Fleet Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

Fox’s Fleet values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, submit a reservation request, contact us, or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or services:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Reservation details, including vehicle choice, pickup date, pickup time, drop-off date, and drop-off time
  • Any additional information you provide through forms or direct communication
  • Basic website usage information such as browser type, device type, and pages visited

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to reservation requests
  • Confirm availability and booking details
  • Coordinate vehicle pickup, drop-off, and delivery
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service-related updates about your reservation
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Maintain internal business records

3. SMS Communications

If you choose to opt in, Fox’s Fleet may send you non-marketing text messages related to your reservation request, booking updates, pickup or delivery coordination, and customer support.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages. You can reply HELP for assistance.

4. Sharing of Information

Fox’s Fleet does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. This language aligns with GHL’s current A2P approval guidance.

We may share information only when necessary with service providers that help us operate our website, manage communications, or support our business, and only to the extent needed for those services.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to improve site functionality, understand visitor behavior, and support advertising and analytics efforts.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical steps to protect the personal information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the personal information you submitted
  • Ask that we update or correct your information
  • Ask that we delete your information, subject to legal or operational requirements
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

Fox’s Fleet may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
